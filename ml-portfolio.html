Artifact 1: Professional Assessment

📌 Artifact Title

Leadership Growth in AI/ML and Change Management

Introduction

This artifact was developed from my Workshop One professional assessment work. It presents my reflection on two self-assessments focused on AI/ML leadership and change management. I selected it as my first portfolio artifact because it provides a strong starting point for showing who I am as a learner and emerging professional.

Artifact Description

Objective

To evaluate my current strengths and growth areas related to AI/ML leadership and change management.

Process

I completed both self-assessments, reviewed my results, compared common themes across the tools, and wrote a reflection identifying strengths, growth areas, and possible revisions to the assessments.

Tools and Technologies Used

Course-provided self-assessment forms, workshop readings, and written reflection.

Artifact-Specific Value Proposition

  • Unique Value: This artifact shows self-awareness, communication strength, openness to innovation, and a willingness to improve through structured reflection.
  • Relevance: It aligns with my broader value proposition by showing that I approach AI/ML not only as a technical field, but also as one that requires responsible leadership and thoughtful communication.

Customization for Audience

  • Adaptations Made: I organized the artifact around professional growth, leadership readiness, and audience relevance instead of presenting it only as a classroom exercise.
  • Relevance: These choices make the artifact more meaningful to a professional reader who wants to understand my mindset, development, and potential.

Reflection

Significance

I chose this artifact because it demonstrates how I assess myself honestly and connect learning to future professional growth.

Lessons Learned

I learned that communication and adaptability are clear strengths for me, but I also need stronger experience in areas such as risk management, scaling AI/ML initiatives, and applying formal leadership frameworks.

Feedback and Revisions

Feedback

After reviewing the rubric and professional portfolio template, I recognized that the artifact needed stronger audience focus, clearer value-proposition language, and a more professional structure.

Revisions

I clarified the artifact title, expanded the explanation of audience and relevance, organized the content according to the required template sections, and strengthened the explanation of why this artifact belongs in a professional portfolio.
